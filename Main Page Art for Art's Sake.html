ART FOR ART'S SAKE

SEATTLE ART MUSEUM

Seattle Art Museum presents to you... Art for Art's Sake!

The Seattle Art Museum has been wanting to have the opportunity to present this once in a lifetime artwork here in Seattle for all to see. Leonardo Da Vinci is one of the best known artists ever, and his best known piece of art, the Mona Lisa, will be here, in Seattle, on display for the first time ever! Among the elephant in the room, the Mona Lisa, many other pieces of Renaissance artwork, especially Leonardo Da Vinci's artwork, will be on display. Come to see this because this may be your only chance! Pre-order tickets start sale December 1, and are going quick, be sure to buy one now to avoid the long lines!
